Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a medical exam to qualify? expand_more
No, most of our final expense plans require no medical exams or blood tests. You simply answer a few basic health questions on the application.
How soon does my coverage start? expand_more
Depending on the carrier and your health profile, many plans offer "Day One" coverage, meaning your full benefit is active immediately upon policy approval.
What exactly does "final expense" cover? expand_more
These policies are designed to cover funeral costs, medical bills, or any outstanding debts, ensuring your family isn't left with a financial burden.
How much does a typical plan cost? expand_more
Premiums start as low as $9/month. Your actual rate depends on your age, gender, and health status, but we always shop for the most competitive rate available.
Can I get coverage with pre-existing conditions? expand_more
Yes. We work with carriers that specialize in high-risk cases, offering "guaranteed issue" plans for those with serious health concerns.
