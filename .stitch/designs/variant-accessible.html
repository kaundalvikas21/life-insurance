Frequently Asked Questions

Is a medical exam required to apply? +
No. Most of our final expense plans require no medical exams or blood tests. You simply answer a few basic health questions.
How quickly does coverage start? +
Many plans offer day-one coverage, meaning your full benefit is active immediately upon policy approval and first payment.
What does final expense insurance cover? +
These policies cover funeral costs, medical bills, and outstanding debts — ensuring your family isn't left with a financial burden.
How much does a typical plan cost? +
Premiums start as low as $9/month. Your actual rate depends on age, gender, and health — we always find the most competitive available.
Can I apply with pre-existing conditions? +
Yes. We work with carriers specializing in high-risk cases, offering guaranteed issue plans for those with serious health concerns.
